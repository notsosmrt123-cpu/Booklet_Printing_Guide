B. Configure Printer Settings for Epson ET-15000

  1. Go to `File > Print` and select your Epson ET-15000.
  2. Click Printer Properties.
  3. On the Main tab, find the 2-Sided Printing option.
  4. Select the Booklet setting.
  5. Ensure the binding is set to Short-Edge Binding. This is critical.
  6. Click OK.
  1. Go to `File > Print` and select your Epson ET-15000.
  2. Below the Orientation settings, find the dropdown menu (often says `Copies & Pages`).
  3. Select Layout.
  4. For Two-Sided, select Short-Edge binding.
  5. Proceed to print.

C. Perform a Test Print

Before printing the whole document, print just the first sheet. In Windows, type `p1,s1` in the Pages box. On macOS, use the "Open in Preview" method and print only the first sheet.

IV. Troubleshooting Common Issues

Even with careful setup, you might run into issues. This section covers the most common problems and their solutions.

The back of my pages are printing upside down.▼

This is the most common issue. You must select "Flip on short edge" or "Short-Edge Binding" in your printer's properties. "Long-Edge Binding" will print the backs upside down.

My margins are incorrect or look different when printed.▼

Some printer drivers override Word's settings. Check for options like "Scale to fit paper." Also, confirm you've selected the identical paper size in Word and in your printer properties.

The page order is completely wrong.▼

Go back to Layout > Page Setup and confirm that "Book fold" is still selected. If this setting is changed, Word will not impose the pages correctly for booklet printing.

I have extra blank pages at the end.▼

This is expected if your page count isn't a multiple of 4. Word automatically adds blank pages to complete the final sheet for printing.
